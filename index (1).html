Purdue Undergraduate Rocket Propulsion Lab  ·  Jan 2025 – Present

Parametric Accelerator Grid — Ion Thruster

Led the mechanical design of the accelerator grid assembly for an ion thruster. The grid features a hexagonal honeycomb aperture pattern designed to maximize ion transparency while maintaining structural rigidity under high thermal and electromagnetic loads.

  • Designed a parametric accelerator grid in Fusion 360 using hexagonal honeycomb logic and tangent constraints — cutting design iteration time by 20%.
  • Conducted material trade studies on CTE, yield strength, and magnetic permeability; selected 316L stainless steel for optimal electromagnetic and thermal performance.
  • Designed mechanical integration strategies for mounting charged grids ensuring structural integrity and electrical isolation.

IEEE Racing @ Purdue  ·  August 2025 – Present

Electric Kart — Chassis & Steering

Contributed to mechanical design and structural analysis of the electric kart's chassis and steering system, optimizing vehicle dynamics through mass repositioning, component design, and FEA-validated structural analysis.

  • Modeled and optimized 3D chassis components in Fusion 360, contributing to a 10% overall weight reduction.
  • Performed Center of Gravity analysis to reposition battery pack and powertrain — achieving a 45/55 front-to-rear weight bias, improving cornering performance.
  • Designed steering linkage components including limit switch bracket and coupler for precise mechanical integration.
  • Conducted FEA simulations to validate structural integrity and aerodynamic efficiency of the frame.
